Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Location data for weather information
  • Language preferences
  • Temperature unit preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide weather-based outfit recommendations
  • Improve our service and user experience
  • Maintain your preferences across sessions

3. Data Storage

Your preferences are stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not store your data on our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenWeather API for weather data
  • Google Fonts for typography
